Our Journey

Founded in 1994, InterLoom began as a boutique weaving workshop with a singular goal: to build high-quality, long-lasting textiles that honor the environment. Over the decades, we've scaled our operations without losing our artisanal soul.

Today, we proudly supply sustainably sourced cotton, intricate jacquards, and performance smart-textiles to global fashion houses and eco-conscious creators alike.

What Drives Us

The pillars that hold our threads together.

01. Traceability

Every single thread has a birth certificate. We partner directly with ethical farmers.

02. Eco-Innovation

From closed-loop water systems to solar-powered looms, sustainability is stitched into our DNA.

03. Uncompromised Quality

We blend legacy artisanal techniques with microscopic quality checks to guarantee longevity.
